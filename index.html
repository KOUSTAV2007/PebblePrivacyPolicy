Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 13, 2025

Pebble Notes does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

All notes are saved locally on your device and are never uploaded to any server.

We respect your privacy completely. The app is free and contains no ads, analytics, or third-party trackers.

What this means for you

  • Your notes stay on your device — nothing leaves your phone or tablet.
  • There is no account required and no cloud sync by default.
  • We do not use analytics or advertising SDKs that collect user information.

Permissions

Pebble Notes requests only the permissions necessary for local features (for example, storage access to save exported notes). These permissions are used solely on-device and not shared.
